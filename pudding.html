Pudding

Description

In 2-quart saucepan, mix sugar, cornstarch and salt. Gradually stir in milk. ... Gradually stir at least half of the hot mixture into egg yolks, then stir back into hot mixture in saucepan. Boil and stir 1 minute; remove from heat. ... Pour pudding into dessert dishes. Cover and refrigerate about 1 hour or until chilled.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • cornstarch
  • salt
  • milk
  • egg yolks, slightly beaten
  • tablespoons butter, softened
  • vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine the first three ingredients
  2. Add the milk and gently cook
  3. Lower heat and stir
  4. Carefully add the egg yolks
  5. Heat things up again
  6. Bring on the vanilla
  7. Bring on the chocolate
  8. Enjoy!

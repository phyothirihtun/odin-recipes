Fried Rice

Description

Turn your skillet to medium high heat and pour sesame oil into the bottom of your skillet and add the onion, peas and carrots. Fry this until they are tender.
Pour the beaten eggs onto the other side. Use a spatula to scramble the eggs. Mix them together with the veggies

Ingredients

  • Small white onion
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. Turn your skillet to medium high heat and pour sesame oil into the bottom of your skillet and add the onion, peas and carrots. Fry this until they are tender.
  2. Pour the beaten eggs onto the other side. Use a spatula to scramble the eggs. Mix them together with the veggies.
  3. Add the rice. Combine it with the veggie and egg mixture. Pour the soy sauce on top and stir until it is heated throughout. Garnish with green onions.
